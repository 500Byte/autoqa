# Product

## Register

product

## Users
QA Engineers and developers who need to perform automated audits on SEO, accessibility, and link integrity. They are technical users who prioritize precision, data accuracy, and clear paths to resolution.

## Product Purpose
AutoQA exists to provide a high-fidelity, functional environment for technical site auditing. It aims to replace manual, fragmented testing workflows with a unified interface that delivers substantially complete and functional reports.

## Brand Personality
Precise, Insightful, Efficient. The tool should feel like an expert companion that doesn't hide complexity but organizes it perfectly.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS dashboard templates, cluttered enterprise interfaces with low data density, and "AI slop" patterns like inconsistent spacing or purely decorative glassmorphism.

## Design Principles
- **Data-First Clarity**: Hierarchy is dictated by the severity and fixability of the audit results.
- **Precise Attribution**: Every issue found must be clearly linked to its source (URL, DOM node, or DNS record).
- **Expert Efficiency**: The UI should favor fast navigation and batch operations for power users.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Targeting WCAG 2.1 AA as a baseline, given the product itself is an accessibility auditing tool. High contrast, keyboard navigability, and clear focus states are non-negotiable.
